Review Strategy

Getting more reviews, with less friction

Your current setup sends two emails with a 10% discount and gets the job done. But without a dedicated review platform, it is limited by the accuracy of Shopify's native triggers and the friction of sending customers to an external page. Migrating to Judge.me removes both of those ceilings.

Current setup
  • 2-email review request sequence
  • 10% discount incentive on both emails
  • Triggered at order fulfilment, no advanced filtering
  • No product-specific targeting
Proposed changes
  • Migrate to Judge.me for native Shopify integration, more accurate delivery triggers, and higher review submission rates
  • Product-specific review requests for key SKUs, so high-performing products build social proof faster
  • Zaymo integration (optional) enables customers to submit a review directly within the email, removing the need to click through to an external page
  • WhatsApp review requests once the WhatsApp channel is live, for even higher open and response rates
  • Dedicated review campaigns sent to past customers for higher review volume
Why Judge.me

Judge.me is free and connects natively to Shopify. It triggers review requests based on actual fulfilment events rather than estimated delivery dates, which means better timing and higher conversion. The upgrade is invisible to your customers. Tool cost: free. Zaymo pricing is not publicly listed and will be confirmed before any integration work begins.

Wonderment Shipping Flow

Every fulfilment milestone. On-brand, accurate, automated.

The post-purchase window is one of the highest-engagement periods in the customer lifecycle. Wonderment pulls live fulfilment data directly from Shopify, so every notification fires on the actual event, not an estimated date. The result is more accurate communication, higher open rates, and fewer customer support queries.

Why Wonderment

Standard Shopify notifications fire on estimated dates. Wonderment fires on real data. That means Email 04 only goes out when the order is genuinely delivered, making the cross-sell timely rather than premature. It also provides a branded tracking page, keeping customers engaged with QYNDA's brand through the entire fulfilment window.
